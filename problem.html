The Problem

Understanding the challenges in modern rice farming

1. High Costs of Fertilizers and Seeds

Farmers spend a large portion of their income on chemical fertilizers and seeds, reducing profits.

2. Poor Soil Quality Due to Chemical Overuse

Long-term use of synthetic inputs depletes the soil's natural nutrients and structure.

3. Increasing Labor Demands

Manual planting, fertilizing, and pest control require intensive labor and time.
