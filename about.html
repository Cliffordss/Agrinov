About Us

Meet the team behind AgriNova

The Team

We are the Introboyz, a team of undergraduate students passionate about sustainable agriculture.

Our Goal

Create low-cost, eco-friendly solutions for farmers using local resources and practical design.

Our Story

Born out of our entrepreneurship class, AgriNova aims to solve real problems rice farmers face daily: high costs, degraded soil, and labor burden.
